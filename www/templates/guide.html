Eating well is vital for people with cystic fibrosis (CF) below is a general guideline.

General Dietary Guidelines


    1. 3 meals, 2-3 snacks per day
    2. Pancreatic enzyme and vitamin supplementation
    3. Unrestricted diet (high fat foods and additives)
    4. Supplements and nourishments
    5. Encourage variety of while grains, nuts, fruits and veggies
    6. Counseling to provide ideas for calories boosters
    7. Extra salt especially during hot weather or exercise
    8. Adequat Calcium and fat-solube vitamins
